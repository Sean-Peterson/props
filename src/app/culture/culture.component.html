Who We Are

Brew Beer. Drink Beer. Have Fun Doing it!


(Pictured above: Nathan Vannatter, Mike Kee, Brian O'Neill, and Travis Peterson)

Mike, Nate, and Travis spent most of their lives serving in the USAF. Mike and Nate flew special operations missions in various prop planes throughout the world. Travis was a helicopter door gunner/flight engineer in various types of Special Operations helicopters. Our brewery, and many of our beers, gets its name from their shared experiences in propeller aircraft. The last, but not least, owner Brian is a legendary restauranteur with decades of experience from Boston, MA. They are great guys and the ones that make our brewery run. If you see them then say hi! They are as friendly as they are hard-working.
