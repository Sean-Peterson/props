2018 distributed series

Our Distributed Series can be found in bars, package shops, and grocery stores anywhere between Mobile, AL to Appalachicola, FL and all the way up to Huntsville, AL.

Beers On Tap

Blonde Bomber - Medium-bodied American blonde ale with a light color and a balanced hop/malt combination. This unfiltered and unpasteurized ale finishes clean and dry.

Dos Pilotos Cerveza - South American style lager that is brewed with pale malts sourced from Patagonia, Chile. Dos Pilotos is an all day drinking beer that pairs perfectly with a lime.

Flying Coffin IPA - Medium-bodied, balanced IPA that is copper in color with caramel flavors and distinct piney hop qualities. This IPA is infused with Chinook hops and dry-hopped with a Mt. Hood and Cascade hop combination.

4 Kings Brown Ale - 4 Kings is rich in flavor and is seperated from its English counterparts by its slight citrus accent, bitterness, and dry finish.

Prop Oil Porter - Pours black with a light tan colored head. This porter has a substantial, complex, and flavorful roast character that tastes of dark chocolate and roasted grains. A fan favorite with it's characteristics of being not too sweet nor too bitter with a smooth aftertaste.

Luckey O'Neill Lager - Vienna style lager. It's light golden-brown coloring combines with a lager's smooth taste which give this lager an all day lawnmower beer feel.

Twin Prop Double IPA- Take our Flying Coffin and feed it steroids. Our double IPA is boiled for 90 minutes with Zythos hops. Then we dry hop it with a combination of Chinook and more Zythos hops. Twin Prop is robust with sublte "hot" alcohol character and a hop profile that might rip out your tongue.

Black Widow IPA - Dark in color and colorful in flavor. The experience starts with the bite of an IPA and then is quickly balanced out with the smooth flavor of roasted malts.

Ghost Rider Oatmeal Stout - Our stout is made with Patagonia Black Peral Malt. This gives it a low roast taste with mild bitterness. We then add oats which increase the smoothness due to the high content of proteins, lipids, and gums.

Rye of the Tiger - A version of Bier de Garde is made primarily with the rye base malt which gives it a malty body and sweet finish.

Pumpkin Bomb Ale - Our Pumpkin Ale is released seasonally. We brew it with pumpkin puree, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, and nutmeg to bring out the spices of the season.

Jovie Ale - A Christmas season classic. Jovie has a nutty, roasted malt profile that is accented with cloves, cinnamon, and honey. Jovie will keep you warm in the cold months with its 6.1% ABV.
